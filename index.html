Who

I am a hardware automation engineer at NVIDIA working on developing hardware and software systems for GPUs, focusing on thermal management solutions that optimize performance and enhance energy efficiency in high-performance computing systems. I collaborate with Research Scientists at NVIDIA to profile and understand energy performance on Tegra SoC under various robotic AI workloads; Vision Language models, multi-sensory workloads, etc. I am also an aspiring PhD Student with research interests in novel microarchitecture for accelerated inference on edge systems used in robotics, autonomous systems, and IoT. I previously explored Machine learning assisted energy modeling, further detailed in my paper "A novel approach for optimizing building energy models using machine learning algorithms"

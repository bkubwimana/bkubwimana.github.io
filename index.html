Who

I am a Senior Software Engineer developing hardware and software systems for GPUs and Switch ASICs, with a focus on Cumulus OS, hardware management modules, and research to optimize performance and energy efficiency in HPC systems. I collaborate with researchers at NVIDIA, UC Berkeley, and Stanford on optimization techniques for model deployment on edge devices. My current studies explore robotic AI workloads, including reasoning LLMs, VLMs, and multi-sensory inference. My research interests center on hardware–software co-design architectures for accelerated edge inference in autonomous systems.
